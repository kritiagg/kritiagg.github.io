About Me

I'm a Senior Staff Applied Scientist at Hippocratic AI, leading GenAI and Deep Learning research on Polaris—a safety-focused AI system designed specifically for healthcare. My work combines cutting-edge generative AI innovation with real-world clinical validation, having evaluated systems with over 1,100 nurses and 130+ physicians.

Previously, I spent 5+ years at Microsoft's Turing team, where I co-authored foundational multimodal models including BEiT-3, VLMo, and Kosmos-1. Before that, I built security automation frameworks at Adobe, analyzing vulnerabilities across 700+ products.

My research spans healthcare AI, LLM safety, multi-modal learning, and computer vision—with a focus on building trustworthy systems that solve real problems.

Publications

Selected work in AI safety, healthcare AI, and multimodal learning

RED QUEEN: Safeguarding LLMs Against Concealed Multi-Turn Jailbreaking

ICLR 20252024

Proposed RED QUEEN ATTACK achieving 87.62% success rate on GPT-4o and RED QUEEN GUARD reducing attacks to <1%. First comprehensive multi-turn jailbreak study with 56K attack data points across 40 scenarios.

Y. Jiang, K. Aggarwal, T. Laud, K. Munir, J. Pujara, S. Mukherjee

Paper Code

HEART: A Unified Benchmark for Assessing Humans and LLMs in Emotional Support Dialogue

arXiv2026

First framework directly comparing humans and LLMs on multi-turn emotional support across 5 dimensions. Frontier LLMs match ~80% of human empathy but humans excel at adaptive reframing.

Paper

Perfecting Human-AI Interaction at Clinical Scale

arXiv2026

Production framework from 115M+ live patient-AI interactions and 7K+ clinician testing (500K+ test calls). Uses paralinguistics, turn-taking, and escalation markers for safety signals.

Paper

Polaris: A Safety-Focused LLM Constellation Architecture for Healthcare

arXiv2024

1T parameter constellation of cooperative LLM agents for real-time patient conversations. Evaluated by 1,100+ nurses and 130+ physicians. First comprehensive clinician evaluation of healthcare LLM.

S. Mukherjee, P. Gamble, M. Sanz Ausin, N. Kant, K. Aggarwal, et al.

Paper

BEiT-3: Image as a Foreign Language

CVPR 20232023

General-purpose multimodal foundation model using Multiway Transformers. SOTA on vision and vision-language tasks. Treats images as "Imglish" for unified masked language modeling.

W. Wang, H. Bao, L. Dong, J. Bjorck, Z. Peng, Q. Liu, K. Aggarwal, et al.

Paper

Kosmos-1: Language Is Not All You Need

NeurIPS 20232023

Multimodal LLM perceiving general modalities, learning in context (few-shot), and following instructions (zero-shot). Trained on web-scale multimodal corpora with strong generalization.

Paper

VLMo: Unified Vision-Language Pre-Training with Mixture-of-Modality-Experts

NeurIPS 20222022

Unified vision-language model with Mixture-of-Modality-Experts (MoME) Transformer. Jointly learns dual encoder and fusion encoder for flexible vision-language understanding.

H. Bao, W. Wang, L. Dong, Q. Liu, O.K. Mohammed, K. Aggarwal, et al.

Paper

Bletchley: Bootstrapping a Multilingual Multimodal Dataset

ACML 20232023

High-quality multilingual image captions using English captions + translation pairs. SOTA on multilingual COCO, Multi30k, IGLUE, and xFlickr datasets.

O.K. Mohammed, K. Aggarwal, Q. Liu, S. Singhal, J. Bjorck, S. Som

Paper
